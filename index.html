Financial Valuation

We can determine the present value of future cash flows using the following formula:

$$ PV = \sum_{t=1}^{n} \frac{CF_t}{(1+r)^t} $$

Where:

  • $PV$ = Present Value
  • $CF_t$ = Cash Flow at time $t$
  • $r$ = Discount Rate
This discounted cash flow model is fundamental to our valuation process, helping us assess the long-term value of our assets.
